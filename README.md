# AI Data Center Growth & PC Component Price Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of rapid AI data center expansion on the pricing trends of major PC components between 2023 and 2026. Using historical market data, the study explores how increasing demand for AI hardware, GPUs, memory modules, and semiconductor manufacturing capacity influences consumer PC component prices.

The project combines **Excel-based data analysis** and **Power BI interactive dashboards** to identify trends, correlations, and market insights.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze PC component price fluctuations from 2023–2026.
* Examine the relationship between AI data center growth and hardware demand.
* Identify components most affected by AI infrastructure expansion.
* Visualize pricing trends using Power BI dashboards.
* Generate actionable insights through data-driven analysis.

---

## Key Insights

* AI data center expansion significantly increased demand for high-performance GPUs.
* Memory and storage component prices experienced noticeable fluctuations during peak AI infrastructure growth periods.
* Supply chain constraints amplified hardware price volatility.
* Strong positive correlation exists between AI hardware demand and selected PC component prices.
